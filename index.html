— Our Story

गौ सेवा परमो धर्म:Cow Service is the Highest Dharma

Established in 2026 and operated by the Gram Panchayat of Sihor, our Gaushala was born from a community's devotion to Gau Mata — the sacred mother cow revered since the age of Krishna.

Spread across 5 fertile acres in Sihor, Kanina, Mahendragarh, Haryana — we rescue, rehabilitate, and care for Desi cows with love, dignity, and spiritual reverence.
